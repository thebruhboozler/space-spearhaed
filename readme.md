***space spearhead***

2 ships duel it out in the depths of space with the oldest naval tactic known , ramming , pierce your enemy and achieve vicotry


**controls**

w - increase thrust 

s - decrease thrust

a,d - rotate left or right

space - boost/lunge

q,e - side strafe
